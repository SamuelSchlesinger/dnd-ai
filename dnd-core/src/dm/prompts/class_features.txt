## Class Feature Awareness

When players attempt actions related to their class abilities, RECOGNIZE the intent and OFFER or USE the appropriate feature. Don't just narrate - engage the mechanics.

### Barbarian
- Player rages or gets angry → Activate **Rage** (bonus damage, resistance to physical damage)
- Player in danger at low HP → Remind about **Relentless Endurance** (if half-orc) or rage benefits
- Reckless attack available → Offer **Reckless Attack** for advantage (enemies get advantage too)

### Bard
- Player helps/encourages ally → Offer **Bardic Inspiration** (give inspiration die)
- Player performs or entertains → Call for **Performance** check
- Player tries to recall lore → **Jack of All Trades** applies to ability checks

### Cleric
- Player heals someone → Ask which spell: **Cure Wounds**, **Healing Word**, etc. Track spell slots.
- Player prays for guidance → **Divine Intervention** at high levels, or roleplay divine connection
- Player detects evil/good → Offer **Detect Evil and Good** spell if prepared

### Druid
- Player wants to become animal → Offer **Wild Shape** (2 uses per short rest)
- Player wants to talk to animals → Offer **Speak with Animals** spell
- Player examines nature → Automatic knowledge from **Druid** training + Nature check

### Fighter
- Player wants extra action → **Action Surge** (1/short rest)
- Player gets second wind → **Second Wind** (1d10 + level HP, 1/short rest)
- Multiple attacks → **Extra Attack** at level 5+

### Monk
- Patient Defense, Flurry of Blows, Step of the Wind → Track **Ki Points**
- Deflect missiles → **Deflect Missiles** reaction
- Stunning Strike → Offer on hit, costs 1 ki

### Paladin
- Player senses evil → **Divine Sense** (detect celestials, fiends, undead)
- Player heals → **Lay on Hands** (pool of 5 x paladin level HP)
- Player smites → **Divine Smite** on hit (costs spell slot, extra radiant damage)

### Ranger
- Player tracks creatures → **Favored Enemy** bonuses + Survival check
- Player navigates wilderness → **Natural Explorer** benefits
- Player hides → **Hide in Plain Sight** at higher levels

### Rogue
- Player attacks with advantage or ally nearby → **Sneak Attack** damage
- Player avoids trap/spell → **Evasion** (half or no damage on DEX saves)
- Player uses bonus action → **Cunning Action** (Dash, Disengage, Hide)

### Sorcerer
- Player casts spell → Offer **Metamagic** options (if applicable)
- Player wants to cast more → **Font of Magic** to convert sorcery points to spell slots
- Player senses magic → Offer **Detect Magic** if known

### Warlock
- Player communes with patron → Roleplay **Pact** connection with atmospheric patron response
- Player casts spell → Track **Pact Magic** slots (all same level, recover on short rest)
- Player invokes eldritch power → **Eldritch Invocations** benefits

### Wizard
- **IMPORTANT: Arcane Recovery works on SHORT REST** (once per long rest limit)
- After short rest → Offer **Arcane Recovery** to restore spell slots (up to half wizard level)
- Player examines magic → **Arcane Tradition** school benefits
- Player wants to copy spell → Offer to add to **Spellbook**

### General Reminders
- Track **spell slots** when spells are cast
- Track **limited-use features** (Action Surge, Wild Shape, etc.)
- Mention when features **recharge** on short/long rest
- When a feature would clearly help → PROMPT the player: "Would you like to use [Feature]?"
