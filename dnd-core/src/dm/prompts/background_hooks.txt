## Background-Based Adventure Hooks

Based on the player's background, weave in relevant story hooks to create personal stakes. These are suggestions - improvise freely while keeping the player's history relevant.

### Acolyte
- Your temple has sent urgent word - something threatens the faith
- A heresy is spreading, and you recognize a former mentor among the accused
- Sacred relics have been stolen, and divination points to this region
- A pilgrimage site has been corrupted by dark forces
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** Cultists of an opposing faith have tracked you here and attack on sight

### Charlatan
- A past mark has resurfaced with questions and muscle
- Someone is running your old con - poorly - and it's drawing attention
- A former partner has information you need... for a price
- Your fake identity is about to be exposed in the worst possible way
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** Hired thugs from a past mark burst through the door demanding "payment"

### Criminal
- Your fence was murdered, and the evidence points to you
- A heist opportunity too good to pass up has fallen into your lap
- The law is closing in - someone talked
- A crime lord wants to recruit you for "one last job"
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** Rivals from your old gang kick down the door seeking bloody revenge

### Entertainer
- A rival challenges your fame with a public performance duel
- A powerful patron offers wealth for a private performance... too private
- Your signature piece was stolen and is being performed elsewhere
- A critic who ruined your mentor needs to be dealt with
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** A jealous rival has hired assassins to eliminate the competition permanently

### Folk Hero
- Your village sends desperate word - the tyrant has returned
- Others have taken up your symbol, but their methods are questionable
- The deed that made you famous has consequences you never anticipated
- Common folk recognize you and beg for help you're not sure you can give
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** The tyrant's soldiers have found you and attack to make an example

### Guild Artisan
- A rare material surfaces on the black market - you need it
- Guild politics threaten your standing; a rival has made accusations
- Your masterwork was commissioned by someone dangerous
- An apprentice has gone missing while working on your behalf
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** Thieves break in to steal your latest work and won't leave witnesses

### Hermit
- The discovery that drove you to isolation has new, urgent relevance
- Others have found your former sanctuary - with questions
- The visions that plagued you in solitude are coming true
- Something you left behind in isolation has followed you back
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** The creature that drove you from your sanctuary has tracked you here

### Noble
- Family honor is at stake in a dispute you can't ignore
- Political intrigue draws you into dangerous noble games
- An old family servant has information about your lineage
- Your claim to inheritance is being challenged by unknown rivals
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** Assassins sent by a rival house strike without warning

### Outlander
- Your tribe calls for aid against an ancient enemy stirring
- The land you knew is dying, and you must find out why
- Hunters from your past track something valuable you possess
- Spirits of the wild send warnings only you can interpret
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** A pack of predators from your homeland has followed your scent

### Sage
- A forbidden text surfaces with knowledge you desperately seek
- Your research has led to a dangerous truth someone wants buried
- A scholarly rival has made a breakthrough using your stolen work
- The answer to a lifelong question lies in this very place
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** Agents of a secret society attack to silence your dangerous knowledge

### Sailor
- An old crewmate needs help with a debt they can't pay
- A legendary treasure map has fallen into your possession
- Your former ship has been spotted - but it sank years ago
- Someone from a port you'd rather forget has tracked you down
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** Pirates from your past have cornered you seeking bloody vengeance

### Soldier
- Your old commander sends word of urgent need
- War criminals from your unit have been sighted nearby
- A mission that went wrong has survivors who remember you
- Military secrets you carry have made you a target
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** Enemy soldiers who survived your last campaign have tracked you down for revenge

### Urchin
- Someone from the streets has information about your family
- The crime boss who "owned" your territory wants you back
- A child in trouble reminds you of your younger self
- The hidden places of cities reveal secrets no one else can find
- **[IMMEDIATE THREAT]** Street enforcers from your old territory have come to collect on old debts with violence
